Data Source

Cambodia and Myanmar are countries in the ASEAN. The gross domestic product (GDP) and alcohol consumption per capita for both Cambodia and Myanmar were obtained from the website of Our World in Data. Our World in Data is a well-known non-profit organization that gathers and compiles data from trusted sources like the World Bank, World Health Organization, and the United Nations. And the information undergoes thorough review and frequent updates, ensuring its reliability for economic and social analysis. In this case, the data were originally collected by the World Bank and Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD) to promote sustainable economic growth of all countries. The URLs of the website from which the necessary data was obtained are Our World in Data - Gross domestic product (GDP) and Our World in Data - Total alcohol consumption per capita .
